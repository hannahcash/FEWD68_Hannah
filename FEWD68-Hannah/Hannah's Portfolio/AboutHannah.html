Hannah Cassius

Product Manager/ Ping Pong Master/ Ice Cream Blogger

A woman of many hats, Hannah Cassius is:

  • A New York-based Product manager at the New York
    • Times
  • Chief blogger at Ice Cream O' Clock
  • Ping-pong master extraordinnaire.

At the New York Times, Hannah focuses on developing the next generation of user-centric digital products. Prior to The New York Times, Hannah worked at Foreign Affairs, a magazine published by The Council on Foreign Relations, where she developed their newsletters, events, and worked on their website redesign.

She has a degree from Vassar College, where she also worked as a multimedia developer, and she also used to TA for the General Assembly User Experience class. In her spare time, Hannah enjoys playing ping-pong, lifting weights, and the occasional night watching Netflix with her cat, Dr. Dre.
